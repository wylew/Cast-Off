Title: The Port of Saltmere

Our adventure began in the bustling port city of **Saltmere**, where our party first met at the Rusty Anchor tavern.

## The Party

- **Kaelen** - A mysterious half-elf ranger with a haunted past
- **Mira** - A clever human rogue seeking her fortune  
- **Theron** - A stalwart dwarf cleric devoted to the sea goddess

## What Happened

The session kicked off with a mysterious job offer from Captain Rhogar, who needed skilled adventurers for a delicate mission:

1. Retrieving a stolen artifact from the lighthouse
2. Navigating treacherous waters near the Shattered Isles
3. Dealing with rival pirates who claim the artifact is rightfully theirs

The captain mentioned cryptic words about "when the tide is highest" that would prove significant later.

**Session Highlight**: Mira's incredible deception check (natural 20!) to bluff our way past the harbor guards.
