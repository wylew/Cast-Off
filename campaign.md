# Campaign Details: Ringside of the Sea

Welcome to the **Ringside of the Sea** campaign! This journal serves as a high-level overview of our journey.

Our adventure takes place in the various ports and islands across the vast ocean, where danger lurks beneath the waves and intrigue brews in every tavern.

- **The Whisper Reef**: A treacherous area known for mysterious sirens.
